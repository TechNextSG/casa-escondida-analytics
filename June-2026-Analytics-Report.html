Key Insights & Observations
📈
Impressions Nearly Doubled — Visibility Surge
From 1,105 → 2,134 impressions (+93%). Google is now showing Casa Escondida for significantly more searches. This is a direct result of the SEO improvements made in May — new pages being indexed and gaining traction. Expect this trend to continue into July.
🚀
Clicks Growing — 105 Visitors from Google
+27% more visitors than May (85 → 108). With Week 4 reaching record impressions (771), the momentum going into July is strong. If impression growth continues, we can expect 130–150 clicks in July organically.
📍
Position Creeping Upward
Average position improved from 9.0 → 8.6. Branded keywords ("casa escondida anilao") are ranking in positions 6–7, close to the top-5 threshold where clicks spike dramatically. Continued SEO work should push branded terms into the top 5 by Q3 2026.
⚠️
CTR Dip — Action Needed on Meta Descriptions
CTR dropped from 7.7% to 5.2% because new impressions are for broader queries where the site isn't the obvious result. Improving meta descriptions on the Homepage, Dive page, and Blog posts to be more compelling can recover 1–2pp of CTR, translating to 20+ additional monthly visitors.
🗓️
June 15 — Best Single Day (9 Clicks)
June 15 had the highest single-day click count with 9 visits, matching the high impressions (74). This suggests external traffic drivers (social posts, referrals?) may have boosted that day. Worth checking Google Analytics to identify the referral source.
🔮
July Outlook — Accelerating Momentum
June 30 closed the month at 116 impressions — the highest single day on record. Week 4 impressions (771) were 88% higher than Week 1 (411). If this trajectory holds, July could see 2,500–3,000 impressions and 120–140 clicks. The SEO foundation is paying off.
