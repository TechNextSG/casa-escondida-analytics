Strategic Recommendation · June 2026
Why Casa Escondida Should Consider Facebook Ads Now
Your organic SEO is growing well — impressions nearly doubled in May. But organic growth alone takes 6–12 months to reach significant scale. Facebook Ads can deliver immediate, targeted visibility to qualified divers while SEO continues building in the background.
The Gap We Need to Bridge
85 organic clicks/month is a strong start. But to fill rooms consistently, the resort needs 500–1,000+ qualified visitors per month. Facebook Ads can bridge that gap today — not in 12 months.
Precision Targeting Available
Facebook lets us target by location (SG, HK, PH), interests (scuba diving, PADI, underwater photography, travel), and behaviors (frequent travelers, diving club members). Your ideal guest, directly in their feed.
Results in Days, Not Months
Unlike SEO which compounds over time, a Facebook campaign can drive resort inquiries within 48–72 hours of launch. Ideal for filling specific dates, promoting dive packages, or peak season campaigns.
What The Data Tells Us About Facebook Ads Potential
1
Singapore has a 25% CTR on Google — meaning 1 in 4 Singaporeans who discover you want to visit. Facebook Ads can dramatically increase how many Singaporeans DISCOVER you in the first place, at low cost per click.
2
97% of your Google traffic goes to the homepage only. Facebook Ads can send traffic directly to your Rooms page, Dive packages page, or a dedicated booking landing page — where guests convert.
3
General dive keywords (scuba diving Batangas, anilao dive center) rank on pages 4–6 on Google. Facebook Ads bypass this entirely — you appear in the feeds of people interested in diving, regardless of what they searched.
4
Your visual content is strong — underwater photos, resort shots, BBQ nights. Facebook and Instagram are visual platforms where dive resorts consistently achieve high engagement and low cost-per-inquiry.
