Key Insights — May 2026
1
Strong end-of-month momentum
Clicks grew from 15 in Week 1 to 34 in Week 4 — a 127% increase within the month. Traffic is accelerating, not plateauing.
2
CTR well above industry average
At 7.7% CTR, the site is performing more than 2× the 3–5% industry benchmark — search listings are compelling and relevant to searchers.
3
Singapore is the highest-intent market
Singapore visitors convert at a 25% CTR — over 3× the overall average. This audience is actively researching and highly likely to book.
4
Brand queries drive 51% of all clicks
"Casa escondida" variations account for ~43 of 85 clicks. Generic terms like "scuba diving batangas" sit at position 43 — significant untapped organic traffic potential.
5
Blog content is gaining impressions, not yet clicks
The Manila-to-Anilao guide and Best Dive Sites blog earned 147 combined impressions with 0 clicks. Google is indexing them — stronger meta descriptions will convert views to visits.
6
Desktop users have higher booking intent
Desktop CTR is 9.36% vs mobile's 6.57%. Mobile drives more impressions (609 vs 545) but fewer clicks — mobile page speed and descriptions are the key lever.
